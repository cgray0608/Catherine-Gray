Donating

A great thing about makeup is the confidence it can give someone. Donating lightly used or new makeup to women in tough situations can be incredibly beneficial to their self esteem, and can make them feel special despite their current hardships. Many people tend to have makeup products they just don't end up using for whatever reason. Unfortunately, these items often sit unused and eventually get thrown out. Through donating these products, it's a great way to declutter, and of course to give to someone who maybe can't afford that lipstick.

These are just some of many donations received at Project Beauty Share.

What can I donate?

Some items you can donate are...

  • New and lightly used makeup
  • Skincare items
  • Makeup bags
  • Haircare products
  • Feminine hygeine products

Who Can Help?

One of the best parts about these organizations is that it only takes one person to make a difference. Even if one person donates one lipstick, this is going to go to a woman who may really need that confidence boost while going through some hardships. In addition the the average person, there are some beauty influensers on YouTube who occasionaly pair up with organizations in order to help them raise money. One YouTuber well known for helping out one of these organizations is someone who goes by the name "Thataylaa" on YouTube.

Where can I donate?

Some great places to donate are...

  • Project Beauty Share
  • Your local shelters
  • Beauty Bus
  • Dress for Success
