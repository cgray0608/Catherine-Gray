The True Beauty of Makeup

The true beauty of makeup is in the confidence it can give some people. Those with certain skin conditions such as rosacea and cystic acne can use makeup to cover their imperfections. This allows them to feel as though this allows for people to see just them, and not their skin condition. Many also see makeup as an art form, and love to express themselves through more avant garde looks, some of which are featured under the "Fun With Makeup" tab.
