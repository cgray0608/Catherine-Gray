Makeup Application


Proper makeup application can be very tricky, and can be hard for beginners, as well as people more experienced with makeup. This page will break down the essentials regarding makeup application of things like foundation, lipstick, and eyeshadow.


Foundation

Foundation is one of the hardest makeup products to apply, and can be tricky for any skin type. Different application methods include...

Makeup brushes

Fingers

Makeup sponges

Silicone makeup applicators

Eyeshadow

Dependent on the type of eyeshadow, and where you want to place the product, there are a few ways to apply eyeshadow. These application methods include...

Eyeshadow brushes:
Fluffy brushes can be used to blend shadows, and flat brushes can be used to deposit more product.

Sponge applicators:
Although these get a bad rep, these can actually work very well. These can work well in order to deposit eyeshadow on the eyelid, and alloe for an amazing color pay off.

Fingers:
This method really works best for cream shadows, as well as shimmery or glittery shadows that you want to look very intense. Using a brush for products like these often makes them look dull and washed out.

Lipstick

Lipstick can also be tricky to get the hang of. There are many different types of lip products, just some of them include tube lipsticks in different finishes, as well as liquid lipsticks that dry down matte. Using a small lip brush to apply and clean the edges of the application will really help the lipstick to look nice and clean. Applying regular tube lipstick with a lip brush will also increase the longevity of the wear.
