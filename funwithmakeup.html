Fun With Makeup

Makeup is worn by many and is part of their daily routines. It can be used to hide imperfections, and to accentuate their favorite features. For others, however, they see makeup as more of an artform or a hobby. From Halloween makeup looks, to avant garde looks, there are many talented people who love to share their looks via social media.

A few of these people well known for their incredible makeup skills and talent in regards to makeup are: Kristi from RawBeautyKristi, Mykie from GlamandGore, and Jordan Hanz.
